About Crush Brewing

Crush Brewing was founded by four co-workers/friends/family. We chose the name “Crush Brewing” as a double meaning, the first has to do with the brewing process itself where the first step is to crush the whole grain malt with a mill. The second meaning is to have an intense attraction, which is what we hope our customers will feel for us.

With a Pennsylvania G Brewing license, we manufacture and sell our beer on and off premise. We have a 10-barrel brew house with 3 fermenters and plan to brew 2 days per week for the first year (with four weeks off) for a total of 960 barrels.

Organization and Management

The four founding members which bring a different expertise to our business.
